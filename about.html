About Us

Ninth Wave was started with an objective of being a Technology Leader and Total Integrated Solution Provider. We strive to achieve our goal by continuous innovation and bringing the latest technology in the Indian market to earn the faith of our valued customers. In pursuit to achieve our goals we have continuously made improvements in our internal process.

Our business practices and processes have combined into a singular philosophy of being trusted partners who provide innovative solutions. It’s a philosophy we are proud to live up to….
